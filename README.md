## What Is Proofly?

[Proofly](https://proofly.shipquick.app) is a video testimonial collection and display tool built for indie hackers, SaaS founders, and small teams. It solves a specific, painful problem: **most customers won't record a video testimonial because the process is too much friction.**

Proofly eliminates that friction. Customers click a link → record in their browser → submit. No account. No app. No confusion.

You review the submission, approve it, and your Wall of Love updates automatically.

```
1. You generate a request link (takes ~30 seconds)

         ↓
3. You review the submission in your dashboard
         ↓
4. You approve it → it appears on your Wall of Love
         ↓
5. One <script> tag embeds your live wall on any page
```

## Features

### No-Login Recorder

The most important feature — and the one that actually makes customers follow through.

Customers open a link in any browser on any device. The recorder runs in-browser with no plugins. They can re-record as many times as they want before submitting. Consent is handled automatically with a plain-English screen before recording begins.

**Why this matters:** every extra step in the recording flow reduces completion rate. No login = more testimonials.

### Magic Prompts

Open-ended recording produces bad testimonials ("yeah, it's good"). Proofly surfaces guided prompts on-screen during recording:

- *What problem were you trying to solve?*
- *What changed after you started using it?*
- *Who would you recommend this to?*

The result is a before/after narrative that's actually persuasive — not a blank stare at a camera. You can customize the prompts for your product.

### Twitter/X Import

Import tweets about your product as text testimonials and display them alongside videos on your Wall of Love. Useful for populating your wall before you have video submissions, or for capturing praise that's already out there.

### Wall of Love Embed

```html
<script
  src="https://proofly.shipquick.app/embed.js"
  data-wall-id="YOUR_WALL_ID"
></script>
```

Drop that anywhere. The embed:

- Works with React, Next.js, Shopify, WordPress, Webflow, plain HTML
- Loads asynchronously — doesn't block your page
- Updates automatically as you approve new testimonials
- Supports light/dark mode and custom brand colors

### Unlimited Team Members

Every plan — including free — supports unlimited team members. No per-seat pricing. Relevant if you're an agency or adding collaborators as you grow.

## Quickstart

Get your first video testimonial live in under 10 minutes.

**1. Sign up at [proofly.shipquick.app](https://proofly.shipquick.app)** — no credit card required.

Go to **New Request** in the dashboard. Name it, optionally customize the prompts, copy the link. (~2 min)

```
Hey [Name], quick favor — would you record a 60-second video about 
your experience with [Product]? No signup needed, just click this 
link and record in your browser: [link]

Takes under 2 minutes and would mean a lot.
```

**4. Approve the submission** in your dashboard when it arrives.

**5. Create a Wall of Love** under **Walls**, customize appearance, copy the embed code.

**6. Paste the embed code** into your landing page. Done.
